Turkey Chili

Hearty Chili

A hearty chili with a bit of kick, made with ground turkey but the turkey could be substituted with ground beef.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground turkey
  • 2 cans of Rotel
  • enchilada sauce
  • taco sauce
  • 3 cans of chili beans

Steps

    Cook the turkey thoroughly
    Add everything 1 item at a time, stirring after each item
    Let the chili cook on low heat for 30 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes
